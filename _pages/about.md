I am currently a faculty member at Fuzhou University and received my Ph.D. in Space Physical Geodesy from Delft University of Technology (TU Delft) in the Netherlands. My research mainly focuses on two areas: (1) the theory and applications of the Earth's gravity field, as well as the processing and analysis of satellite geodetic observations; and (2) urban environmental sensing using intelligent devices.

Beyond these topics, I am also broadly interested in exploring novel research ideas and innovative devices. Students and collaborators who are curious, self-motivated, and passionate about research are welcome to get in touch to explore new possibilities together.

现于福州大学工作，博士毕业于荷兰代尔夫特理工大学空间物理大地测量专业。研究主要聚焦两个方向：一是地球重力场理论与应用，以及卫星大地测量观测数据的处理与分析；二是利用智能设备开展城市环境感知研究。此外，我也关注新型科研思路与创新设备的探索。欢迎具有好奇心、主动性和科研热情的学生联系交流，共同探索新的研究课题。
